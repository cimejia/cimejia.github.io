Since 2013, he joined as a professor at the Central University of Ecuador, Quito, Ecuador, being in 2016 appointed as a full-time professor in the areas of Software and Statistics.

His research interests include:
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Machine Learning classical algorithms
  • Deep Learning and Artificial Neural Networks, in particular for computer vision tasks
  • Geostatistical focused on environmental resources

He is currently pursuing a Ph.D. degree in Computer Science at the University of Alicante, Spain.
